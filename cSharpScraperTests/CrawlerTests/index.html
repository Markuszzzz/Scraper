Lindsey Zurbrugg, Wheelchair Basketball: Lindseys journey in para sports began after she sustained a spinal cord injury from an undiagnosed birth defect at the age of 13. Craving the sense of community that team sports provide, and missing the rush of wind blowing through her hair while competing, Lindsey entered the world of Wheelchair Basketball where shes gone on to make her mark earning two collegiate national championships. Lindsey is passionate about bringing para sports to a wider audience who may not realize just how competitive it can be. She believes sports have taught her invaluable life lessons such as the importance of accountability, work ethic, and timeliness. Fun fact: Lindsey was the 2023 #IAmCrackerJill contest winner and will be featured on Cracker Jill速 bags this year.
